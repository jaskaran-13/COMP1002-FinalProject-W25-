Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Game Boy batteries last?

The original Game Boy runs for about 15-30 hours on 4 AA batteries, depending on usage.

Are Game Boy games region locked?

No, Game Boy games are not region locked and will work on any Game Boy system worldwide.

Can I connect my Game Boy to a TV?

The original Game Boy doesn't have TV output, but the Super Game Boy adapter for SNES allows you to play Game Boy games on your television.
